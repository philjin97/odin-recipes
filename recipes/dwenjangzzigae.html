Dwenjangzzigae

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 T + 1 tsp Dwenjang
  • 3 cups of rice water or water
  • 1 handful dried large anchovies
  • 1 piece dried kelp
  • 1 small grey squash or zucchini
  • 1/2 pack of 14 oz extra firm tofu
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • 1/2 tsp red chili pepper power
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 1 green chili pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by saving 3 cups of rice water when rinsing rise
  2. Prepare other ingredients: cut tofu, slice green onion and chop garlic
  3. Add dried soup anchovies and kelp to rice water
  4. Discard anchovies and kelp
  5. Turn off heat and mix in dwenjang
  6. Turn heat to medium high and bring to dwenjang broth to boil
  7. Add squash or zucchini. Boil for 5 min
  8. Add tofu and boil for another 5 min
  9. Add green onions and chopped garlic. Boil for 3 more minutes and serve!
